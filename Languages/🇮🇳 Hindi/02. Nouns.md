## Masculine Nouns

### Type 1 (Marked) Nouns

-   Ends with vowel `рдЖ` _(a)_ in singular
    -   `рдХрдорд░рд╛` _(room)_
-   Ends with vowel `рдП` _(i)_ in plural
    -   `рдХрдорд░реЗ` _(rooms)_

### Type 2 (Unmarked) Nouns

-   All masculine nouns which do not end in `рдЖ`
-   Have the same singular and plural form
    -   `рдШрд░` _(home / homes)_

## Feminine Nouns

### Type 1 (Marked) Nouns

-   Typically end in `рдИ` _(i)_ in singular
    -   `рд▓рдбрд╝рдХреА` _(girl)_
-   Ends in `рдЗрдпрд╛рдБ` _(iyan)_ in plural
    -   `рд▓рдбрд╝рдХрд┐рдпрд╛рдБ` _(girls)_

### Type 2 (Unmarked) Nouns

-   Other feminine nouns which form their plural by suffixing `рдПрдБ` _(en)_
    -   `рдорд╛рддрд╛` _(mother)_
    -   `рдорд╛рддрд╛рдПрдБ` _(mothers)_

# Case [](https://hindilanguage.info/hindi-grammar/nouns/case/)

## Direct case

-   when a nominal phrase is not followed by postposition

# Vocative Case

- ItтАЩs used for direct address
- Has the same form as oblique case, except that the plural forms are not nasalized
