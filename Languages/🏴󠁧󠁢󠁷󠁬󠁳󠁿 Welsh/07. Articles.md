# Articles
- there's no indefinite article in Welsh
- there are three forms of definite article
	- `y`
		- `ci → y ci` *(the dog)*
	- `yr`
		- `ysgol → yr ysgol` *(the school)*
	- `r`
		- `Mae'r plant` *(the children are)*
