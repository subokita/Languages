# Zo'n
- it's used before singular nouns
- it's also often used before plural nouns in colloquial Dutch
	- `Zo'n schattige hond!`
	- *Such a cute dog!*

# Wat and Hoe
- they are used interchangeably
	- `Wat leuk!`
	- *How nice!*
- `hoe` is bit more formal
	- `Hoe leuk!`
	- *How nice!*
