# Non Neuter

-   ends in a consonant (except l, r)
    -   `+en (armen)`
-   ends in vowel
    -   `+n (industrin)`
-   ends in unstressed vowel
    -   `+n (fageln)`

# Neuter

-   ends in consonant
    -   `+et (huset)`
-   ends in vowel
    -   unstressed
        -   `+t (yrket)`
    -   stressed
        -   `+et (geniet)`
