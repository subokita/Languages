# Participles and Supine

## Use of supine

- with har/hade to form perfect/pluperfect tenses
    - `Olle har tv√§ttat bilen`
    - *Olle has washed the car*
    - `Han hade tv√§ttat den innan det b√∂rjade regna`
    - *He had washed it before it began to rain*

## Use of Past Participles

- as an adjective
    - `en nytv√§ttad bil`
    - *a newly washed car*
    - `ett nyk√∂pt bord`
    - *the car is a newly bought table*
    - `en omskriven bok`
    - a rewritten book
    - `den nytv√§ttade bilen`
    - the newly washed car
    - `det nyk√∂pta bordet`
    - the newly bought table
    - `den omskrivna boken`
    - *the rewritten table*

# Present Participle

## Verbs with stem ending in consonant

- Stem + (a)nde
    - `De kom springande`
    - They came running

## Verbs with long vowel

- Stem + ende
    - `Han blev troende`
    - *He remained in faith*
