# Masculine

-   Nouns ending in consonant and -й
    -   `дом (house)`
    -   `музей (museum)`
-   Nouns denoting males ending with -ь
    -   `гость (guest)`
-   Months ending in -ь
    -   `январь (January)`

--- column-end ---

# Feminine

-   Nouns ending in -а -я -ья -ия
    -   `статья (article)`
    -   `машина (car)`
-   Nouns ending in -знь -сть -сь
    -   `жизнь (life)`
    -   `новость (news)`

--- column-end ---

# Neuter

-   Nouns ending in -о -е -ье -ие
    -   `место (seat)`
    -   `здоровье (health)`
