-   For verbs that end with `-cer` and `-cir`, change `c` to `zc` for the `yo` form
    -   `conocer -> yo conozco`
-   For verbs that end with `-ger` and `-gir`, change `g` to `j` for the `yo` form
    -   `escoger -> yo escojo`
-   For verbs that end with `-guir`, change `gy` to `g` for the `yo` form
    -   `seguir -> yo sigo`
