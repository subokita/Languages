# Personal Pronouns

## Singular

| Case       | 1st person 🏛️ | 1st person 🇬🇧 | 2nd person 🏛️ | 2nd person 🇬🇧 |
| ---------- | ------------- | ------------- | ------------- | ------------- |
| Nominative | ego           | I             | tū            | you           |
| Genitive   | meī           | of me         | tuī           | of you        |
| Dative     | mihi          | to me         | tibi          | to / for you  |
| Accusative | mē            | me            | tē            | you           |
| Ablative   | mē            | from me       | tē            | from you      |
